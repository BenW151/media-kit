About Me

My name is Tia, I am the face behind @teetravels_. I am a travel content creator, blogger and videographer who is passionate about sharing my love for travelling. Being a full time traveller I have a very engaged community of followers who trust my expertise in travel related recommendations and come to my page for travel inspiration. I specialise in voice over content as this allows me to truly storytell and convey my passion for where I am travelling, making my content very authentic and relatable.
